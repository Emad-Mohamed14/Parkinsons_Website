Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)

What is Parkinson's?

Parkinson's disease is a progressive neurological disorder that primarily affects movement, causing symptoms like tremors, stiffness, and slow movements.

How can I manage Parkinson's?

Managing Parkinson's disease involves medication, lifestyle changes, and therapies tailored to individual needs and symptoms.

What are the early signs of Parkinson's?

Early signs include tremors, stiffness, slow movement, and difficulty with balance.

Is there a cure for Parkinson's?

There is no cure, but treatments can help manage symptoms effectively.
