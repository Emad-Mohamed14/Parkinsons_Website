Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)

What is Parkinsons?

Parkinson's disease is a progressive neurological disorder that primarily affects movement, causing symptoms like tremors, stiffness, and slow movements, resulting from the loss of brain cells that produce dopamine

How can I manage Parkinsons?

Managing Parkinson's disease involves a multi-faceted approach, including medication, lifestyle changes, and therapies, all tailored to your individual needs and symptoms.
